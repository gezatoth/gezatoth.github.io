Scalable Quantum State Tomography

As the system size is increasing in multi-particle quantum experiments, it becomes more and more difficult to realize full quantum state tomography. Thus, several alternatives have been developed to obtain a density matrix from a tomographic procedure even if a full set of measurements cannot be realized. One of these methods is Permutationally Invariant Quantum Tomography. It is an important subproject of the ERC Starting Grant GEDENTQOPT. This home page describes the basic characteristics of Permutationally Invariant Quantum Tomography, and also collects the publications and the presentations about it.
