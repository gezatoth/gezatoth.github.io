CV of Géza Tóth, Sr. (1930-2005)

Research topic: radiochemistry, radiopharmacology

1941-1949: He studied in the Piarist High School in Debrecen and he also graduated there.

1949–1953: Kossuth Lajos University of Debrecen, Faculty of Natural Sciences, Chemistry.

1953– Research assistant at the Department of Lajos Imre.

1958: Central Research Institute of Physics of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences, Department of Nuclear Chemistry

1958: Doctor of the University.

1964: Isotope Research Institute, Hungarian Academy of Sciences, Department of Inorganic Chemistry.

1968: Candidate of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences (this is type of doctoral degree).

1979–1993: Department head, Department of Radiopharmacy.

He was a part-time assistant professor at the Department of Chemical Technology, Faculty of Chemical Engineering, Budapest University of Technology and Economics for two decades.

For a long time he was the secretary of the Radiochemical Committee of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences. During this period, the chairman of the committee was Géza Schay.

1988: Prize of the Hungarian Academy of Sciences.

1993: retired.
